We securely hold the details you give us (like your name and email) as well as information from third-party logins (such as your Google avatar). This is necessary to manage your account and tailor the BedReads experience to you.

Story Content

The text you write or input is kept in our system to help us evaluate and upgrade our platform. Please note that to maintain quality and refine our service, site administrators may review story content, even if you have marked it as "private."

Image Uploads

If you upload pictures to customize your stories, please strip out any embedded location tracking (like EXIF GPS) beforehand. Anyone accessing the platform might be able to download the image and extract that location info. We promise to use your uploaded images strictly for story customization and nothing else.
